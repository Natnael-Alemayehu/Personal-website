The NHS Leadership Conundrum

by STEVEN on November 26, 2015, 0 comments

I’m pleased to announce that on 1st October 2016 I became GP Principal at Silverbirch Medical Practice in Bangor, Co.Down. I am taking over the Practice from Dr Nigel Majury who founded it 25 years ago and built it up over the course of his career, providing an excellent service to his patients. Dr Majury is much loved and respected by his patients and I’m thankful to him for entrusting the Practice and his patients to me and look forward to many more years of delivering quality primary medical services to patients in Bangor. I am also planning to provide additional enhanced medical services and online patient services so watch this space for further developments. A dedicated practice website will also be coming soon.
