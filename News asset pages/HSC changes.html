HSC Change Day 2015

by STEVEN on November 26, 2015, 0 comments

On 11 March 2015 I joined with members of the HSC Change Day Core Team, frontline healthcare workers, patients, students, Chief Nursing Officer Charlotte McArdle, Deputy Chief Medical Officer Dr Anne Kilgallen and members of the Health Committee to celebrate the 2nd HSC Change Day.

The day was marked by 40 celebration events across Northern Ireland with hundreds of people joining in via social media using Twitter and Facebook.

If you haven’t pledged yet it’s not too late – go to www.hscchangeday.com/pledge and make your pledge today.
