Strategies to Implement Change in the HSE

by STEVEN on November 26, 2015, 0 comments

I was delighted to be invited by Fresenius Kabi to speak at the Irish Society for Clinical Nutrition and Metabolism (IrSPEN) Satellite Symposium on ‘Strategies to Implement Change in the HSE’ on Tuesday 10th March in Dublin.

I was speaking alongside Professor Zena Moore, Head of the School of Nursing, Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland.
